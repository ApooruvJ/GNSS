At present, this organisation is doing various activities.

At Charity School(Nai kiran Universal School):

Free Education

At present about 200 poor children are getting free education in the school run by the trust.

Free Mid Day Meals

In order to take care of their malnutrition, the children are provided free mid day meals besides clean drinking water.

Admission to Reputed Schools

Various Students have been admitted to various sreputed schools.

At Charitable Hospital (Nai Kiran hospital):

Nai Kiran Eye Hospital:

For continued Medicare of the poor/needy persons, the trust decided to set up a chartable eye hospital in a remote area, to provide medical facilities to the poor patients at there door steps.

Free OPD

A weekly OPD is conducted for patients in the hospitals every Thursday where patients are provided free medicines besides free consultation.

Our Students success:

A lot of our meritorious students have been aceepted in schools, like Shalom Hills, where their fees is paid by the schools themselves.
